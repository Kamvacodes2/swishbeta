PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
Product Name

SWISH

Tagline (working options):

Switch on stronger teams

Where teams connect, grow, and move together

Purpose-built team building for organisations that matter

1. Product Vision

SWISH is a team-building platform designed for corporates, churches, and large groups to design, manage, and experience structured team-building activities that strengthen connection, communication, and shared purpose.

SWISH should:

Feel credible and professional for corporates

Feel warm, inclusive, and values-driven for churches

Scale smoothly for large groups

Support self-service booking and facilitated experiences

Offer clear admin control for organisations and platform owners

2. Target Users & Personas
2.1 Front-End Users
A. Corporate Decision Maker

Examples: HR Manager, Team Lead, People & Culture Manager

Goals

Improve team cohesion

Run structured team-building sessions

Measure engagement and outcomes

Pain Points

Unstructured team events

Hard-to-coordinate logistics

No measurable impact

B. Church / Ministry Leader

Examples: Pastor, Youth Leader, Small Group Coordinator

Goals

Build unity and trust

Encourage participation

Align activities with values

Pain Points

Low engagement

Disconnected groups

Limited tools for managing people

C. Participant (End User)

Examples: Employee, Church member, Volunteer

Goals
